About

CRS system is applicationthat was created to provide companies with an in-depth analysis of their phone calls. Rather than using computers to transcribe and decipher recorded phone calls, we provide working opportunities for tens of thousands of people all over the world just like you. Our reviewers work from home listening to recorded calls, and they provide us with the answers we need to create a meaningful analysis of our clients calls. By combining human intellect with computer generated algorithms, we are able to provide clients with specific reports and statistics to help them make the changes necessary to improve their business
